About Raul

THE MAN BEHIND
THE WRENCH

Raul Tapia has spent over four decades under the hood of diesel trucks — from rebuilding engines roadside to overhauling entire fleets at major trucking operations across the country.

He started at 18, apprenticing under seasoned mechanics who taught him one thing above all else: simplicity wins. A complicated repair nobody understands is worse than no repair at all. That philosophy has defined every mile of Raul's career — and it's the foundation of every class he teaches.

Over the years, Raul has mastered everything from Cummins and Detroit Diesel to Caterpillar, Paccar, and modern DEF/SCR emissions systems. He's diagnosed failures that stumped dealership techs and trained mechanics who now run their own shops.

His gift isn't just knowing the work — it's breaking it down so anyone, regardless of background, can understand it, remember it, and do it confidently on their own.

The Curriculum

WHAT YOU'LL LEARN
IN CLASS

Engine Systems

Tear-down, inspection, and rebuild of diesel engines — Cummins, Detroit, CAT, and Paccar.

Fuel & Injection

High-pressure common rail, mechanical injection, bleeding systems, and injector testing & calibration.

Electrical Diagnostics

Reading wiring diagrams, fault codes, multimeter testing, ECM and sensor troubleshooting.

Air Brakes

Full brake system inspection, adjustment, and repair including slack adjusters and brake chambers.

Emissions / DEF Systems

DPF cleaning, SCR diagnostics, EGR operation, and DEF system repairs to pass inspection.

Transmissions & Driveline

Manual and automatic transmissions, clutch service, driveshafts, and differential basics.
